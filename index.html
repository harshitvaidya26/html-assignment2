About Me

Hi, My name is Harshit Vaidya and I am studying Interactive Media Design (IMD) at Algonquin College CDI in North York, ON, Canada. My academic voyage is inspired by a sparkling zeal for creativity and innovation, aiming towards engaging me in the elementous universe of graphic designing. I am into designing, drums player, soccer, cooking and Hollywood movies etc. It keeps me grounded and energetic whereas designing for me is my passion and motivation to explore creativity at a whole another level!

The dream that I am working towards is to become a graphic designer with the hopes of having my own company in which I do design and content creation for many different clients. I have done a lot in my academic as well as personal life which, frankly speaking now that I see it and think about it, is something to be proud of. There are certifications like the Certified Dassault Systèmes Associate: Mechanical Design — CSWA Certificate that prove my technical knowledge and proficiency with design software. I am also certified in Emergency First Aid with CPR by the Royal Life Saving Society Canada (certified on 26th Feb 2022). The experience of being part of the Delegate Recognition program at the Harvard Model United Nations (HMUN) in India, over a span of 5 days profoundly enriched my leadership, communication and diplomacy skills. I have a bunch of stories that provide me with a unique angle looking into the creative world and all I want to do is use this long list of taught experiences in my future professional graphic design field, producing content.
